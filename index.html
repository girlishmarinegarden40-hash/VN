Credits

Alice Courtyard is a handmade visual novel adaptation built from a long-form original novel, its rewrites, its setting notes, its character voices, and the quiet persistence behind them.

Work Statement

This build exists to present the story in an interactive reading form. Its text, images, music, staging, and interface belong to the project and its creators. Please do not repost, redistribute, mirror, commercialize, extract assets, or republish any part of this build without permission.

Thanks

To every scene that was rewritten until it finally hurt in the right way. To every character who insisted on becoming more than a role. To every image, note, and draft that helped the garden keep growing. And to the readers who step into it carefully enough to let it wake.

Build Note

This is a living web VN build. The novel remains the source, and the staging, audio, CG timing, menus, and presentation can continue to evolve as the garden becomes more complete.
